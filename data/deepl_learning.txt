Deep learning is a subset of machine learning.
It is based on artificial neural networks with multiple layers.
Deep learning models can automatically learn features from large datasets.
Deep learning is widely used in computer vision, speech recognition, and natural language processing.
Neural networks consist of layers of interconnected neurons.
Popular deep learning architectures include convolutional neural networks and recurrent neural networks.
Deep learning requires large amounts of data and powerful computing resources.
Graphics processing units are commonly used to train deep learning models.
Deep learning has enabled major advances in artificial intelligence.
Many modern AI systems are powered by deep learning techniques.
